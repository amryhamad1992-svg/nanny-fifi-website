Meet Sofia

Your Partner in Parenting

Hello, I'm Sofia, known to the little ones as Nanny Fifi! With over 15 years of dedicated childcare experience and a BA (Honours) in Early Childhood from The Open University, I've nurtured children from 4 months to 10 years old, seamlessly integrating with each family's unique culture and needs.

My approach is rooted in comfort, understanding, and gentleness. I believe in treating children as equals, engaging in open dialogue, and empowering them through a child-led approach. Now, I bring this expertise directly to parents through personalised online coaching sessions.
